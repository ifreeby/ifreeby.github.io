5 Top Secret Ways to Get FREE Starbucks Gift Cards

Brittany Kline | Updated April 27, 2020 | Make Money

Want to earn FREE gift cards to your favorite stores? Sign up for Swagbucks now and take advantage of your FREE $5 sign up bonus!

Do you love Starbucks but don’t love how expensive it is? Then we have some good news for you! You can earn free Starbucks gift cards to fund your coffee habit and save money at the same time!

Many people don’t mind paying Starbucks prices – after all, the coffee is amazing and it makes you feel warm, fuzzy, and prestigious. However, Starbucks is expensive and the money really adds up. Why not fund your next trip with a free gift card instead of shelling out $5 just for a small cup of jo?

We compiled a list of our five top-secret ways to earn Starbucks gift cards online. These methods are 100% legitimate and only take a few minutes of your time.

If you want to learn how to score free gift cards to even more places like Amazon, Sephora, and Target, be sure to check out our favorite ways to earn free gift cards here!

Bonus tip: Take earning free Starbucks gift cards a step further. Check out our post on how to earn free internet here!

 

1. Swagbucks

Swagbucks is one of our favorite ways to bring in some extra money and earn free Starbucks gift cards. We especially love the fact that you get a free $5 bonus just for signing up!

Swagbucks is a site that gives you points – or “SBs” – for watching videos, playing games, taking surveys, shopping online, searching the web, and more.

If you have just a few minutes in your day to spare, you can easily earn free Starbucks gift cards or even earn free PayPal money.

Sign up with Swagbucks today and receive a $5 sign-up bonus to put towards your first free Starbucks gift card!

 

2. Survey Junkie

Survey Junkie is another great way to earn free Starbucks gift cards online. It’s one of our favorite survey sites since it pays well compared to other survey sites and is easy to navigate.

Survey Junkie is a site that focuses exclusively on surveys. All you have to do is sign up, fill out your profile, and you’ll be matched with surveys based on your demographics. Each survey tells you how many points you will earn and how long it will take, which is perfect for budgeting your time.

After you take a survey, you are rewarded with points. Once you earn 1,000 points, you can redeem them for cash or free gift cards!

Sign-up for Survey Junkie here and start earning points to put towards your free Starbucks gift cards today!

 

3. Ibotta

Ibotta is a great cashback app that allows you to save money on purchases at the grocery store, department stores, retail stores, clothing stores, and more. We love being able to make some of our money back on purchases we were already going to make!

Here’s how to earn cashback or redeem free Starbucks gift cards with Ibotta.

  1. Download the Ibotta app on your phone.
  2. Open the app and select the products you are going to buy from the store.
  3. Buy the products at the participating store.
  4. Keep your receipt and upload it to the app.

Ibotta will then match your purchases to the receipt and within 48 hours, you’ll have money in your account!

Earn your first free $10 when you sign up with Ibotta through this exclusive link! With that kind of money, you can buy a coffee for yourself and a friend!

 

4. InboxDollars

InboxDollars is very similar to Swagbucks – you can earn money from completing various tasks such as taking surveys, playing games, watching videos, reading emails, surfing the web, and more.

You will earn points for every task you complete. Once you reach the $30 minimum, you can request a payout. InboxDollars has several payout options such as PayPal and free gift cards to Starbucks, Amazon, and Wal-Mart, just to name a few!

Sign up with InboxDollars today and receive a free $5 sign-up bonus!

 

5. Sign Up for a Credit Card

Using credit cards is a great way to earn free gift cards or cashback on purchases if you regularly pay on your cards. You could be making money back on regular purchases at the grocery store, buying gas, paying bills, etc.

If you are sensible with your credit cards, then making purchases on them is a great way to earn rewards such as free gift cards.

For example, you can earn free Starbucks gift cards or free money with Capital One Venture Rewards Credit Card or Chase Sapphire Reserve. All you have to do is make your purchases like normal and make sure you are paying them off on time.

Don’t be tempted to use your card for purchases that you wouldn’t normally make or are outside of your budget!

 

Bonus: How to Get Free Coffee at Starbucks

While it’s not a free gift card per se, the free Starbucks Rewards Program is a great way to earn free coffee at Starbucks.

Once you join, you get some amazing perks, including

  • 2 stars for every $1 spent.
  • Free in-store refills on coffee once you reach 5 stars.
  • Free beverages for every 12 stars after you reach a 30-star minimum.
  • A free food or beverage item on your birthday.

By the way, if you like free stuff on your birthday, then be sure to check out our birthday freebie post. We compiled the ultimate guide to getting birthday freebies!

I don’t know about you, but I get the majority of my coffee at the Starbucks in my Target. I order my coffee and then walk around to do my shopping. Did you know if you purchased the coffee through the rewards program you are eligible to get free refills?

You can drink your first coffee as you are shopping and you can get a refill on your way out! Insert mind-blown emoji! This is seriously a game-changer. This works on hot and iced coffee! #score

 

Final Thoughts

It has never been easier to earn free Starbucks gift cards online! All you need is the internet and a little bit of time and you could be getting free coffee or even free money if that’s what you prefer.

Free gift cards are not only a great way to treat yourself, but make great gifts for family and friends as well.

Did you know about these top-secret ways to earn free Starbucks gift cards? Do you use any other sites to get free gift cards or free money? Let us know in the comments below or share it in our Facebook group!
